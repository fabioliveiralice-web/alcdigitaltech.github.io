Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how ALC Digital Tech handles information in its applications. We value your privacy and comply with Google Play policies.

Last updated: Sep 03, 2025

1. Who we are 2. Data collected 3. Use of data 4. Sharing 5. Ads & analytics 6. Children 7. Security 8. Your rights 9. Retention 10. Changes 11. Contact

1. Who we are

ALC Digital Tech (“ALC”, “we”) develops entertainment and utility mobile apps such as Forca Nordestina, Luz do dia and Jangada Run.. Website: alcdigitaltech.com.br. Email: alcdigital@gmail.com.

2. Data collected

We do not directly request personal data (like name or email). However, third-party SDKs may automatically collect:

  • Device identifiers (e.g. Google Advertising ID)
  • Device and OS information (model, version, language, approximate region)
  • Usage data (sessions, clicks, crashes)
  • Ad interactions for measuring campaigns and preventing fraud

3. Use of data

  • To provide and improve app features
  • To measure performance and fix errors
  • To display ads and keep apps free
  • To prevent fraud and misuse

4. Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Non-identifiable data may be shared with technology providers strictly to achieve the purposes described (for example, AdMob for ads, Crashlytics for stability).

5. Ads & analytics

  • Google AdMob: serves ads using the Advertising ID for personalization (if allowed) and fraud prevention. You can reset or disable ad personalization in your Android settings.
  • Google Play Services / Firebase: may collect usage and stability metrics (e.g. Crashlytics).

More info: Google Ads Policy

6. Children

Our apps are intended for users aged 13+. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us for removal.

7. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect information. However, no transmission or storage system is 100% secure. Please keep your device updated.

8. Your rights

Depending on your region, you may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of data. Please contact alcdigital@gmail.com with the app name and details.

9. Retention

Data collected by third-party services is retained only as necessary for the purposes described and according to their policies.

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The current version will always be available on this page with the “Last updated” date.
